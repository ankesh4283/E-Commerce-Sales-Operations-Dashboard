

# 🛒 E-Commerce Sales & Operations Dashboard

## 📌 End-to-End Business Intelligence & Performance Analytics Project

A comprehensive data analytics project focused on analyzing e-commerce sales performance, profitability, product trends, and operational efficiency. This dashboard transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights to support business growth, cost optimization, and strategic decision-making.

---

## 🚀 Problem Statement

E-commerce businesses often struggle to answer:

* Are we growing in sales but losing profitability?
* Which products drive the highest and lowest revenue?
* Which regions contribute most to total sales?
* How does shipping mode impact overall revenue?
* Which product categories are underperforming?

This dashboard provides clear, data-driven answers.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

To design an interactive dashboard that helps:

* Track Year-to-Date (YTD) sales, profit, and profit margin
* Monitor product-level performance (Top & Bottom products)
* Analyze regional sales distribution
* Evaluate category-wise sales trends
* Understand shipping mode impact on revenue
* Enable segment-level analysis (Consumer, Corporate, Home Office)

# 📈 Key Business Insights

## 🏆 Top Performing Products

* Products like **Staple Envelope** and **Staples** generate the highest revenue

**Insight:**
A small set of products contributes significantly to total sales (Pareto effect).

---

## ⚠️ Bottom Performing Products

* Several products generate extremely low revenue

**Insight:**
Low-performing products may be candidates for removal or optimization.

---

## 📦 Category Performance

* **Office Supplies** leads in total sales
* **Technology** shows slight decline
* **Furniture** shows moderate growth

**Insight:**
Category performance is uneven and needs strategic balancing.

---

## 🌍 Regional Analysis

* **West region** contributes the highest sales (~32%)
* Other regions (East, Central, South) show balanced distribution

**Insight:**
Business is regionally diversified but still dependent on key areas.

---

## 🚚 Shipping Mode Analysis

* **Standard Class** dominates (~60%+ of sales)
* Same-day shipping contributes minimal revenue

**Insight:**
Customers prefer cost-effective shipping over faster delivery.

---

## 📉 Sales vs Profit Trend

* Sales show slight decline
* Profit and margin are improving

**Insight:**
Business is becoming more efficient despite lower sales volume.

---

## 🧩 Segment Analysis

* Consumer segment contributes highest sales
* Corporate and Home Office show lower but stable performance

**Insight:**
Consumer market is the primary revenue driver.

---

## 💼 Strategic Recommendations

* Focus on high-performing products to maximize revenue
* Improve or eliminate low-performing products
* Expand high-performing regions (West & East)
* Optimize shipping strategy (balance cost vs speed)
* Improve performance in Technology category
* Target Corporate & Home Office segments for growth
